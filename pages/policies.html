Shipping Policy

At LogiTrack, we strive to provide reliable and efficient shipping services. Our shipping policy ensures that your packages are handled with care and delivered on time.

  • Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the country
  • International shipping varies by destination (7-14 business days)
  • Express shipping options available for urgent deliveries
  • Free tracking provided for all shipments
  • Insurance coverage available for valuable items

Return Policy

We want to ensure your complete satisfaction with our services. Our return policy is designed to be fair and transparent.

  • Claims must be filed within 48 hours of delivery
  • Damaged items must be reported immediately with photo evidence
  • Full refund for lost packages (terms apply)
  • Compensation for delayed deliveries as per agreement

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring data security.

  • Secure handling of customer information
  • No sharing of personal data with third parties
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Compliance with international data protection regulations
